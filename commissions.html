Commissions

What are commissions?

A commission is when a person, musician, or group asks (often with a commission fee) a composer to write them a piece for a performance, event, movie, etc.


How much do you charge for commissions?

I do not charge anything for commissions at this time because the exposure of having one of my pieces performed is payment enough. Although, if you commission a very large work (i.e. an opera or ballet), I will charge a commission fee because those projects take hundreds of hours on my end to complete.


How do I commission a piece?

To commission a piece, please email me at noahjkthompsonmusic@gmail.com.
In your email please include the following:

  • Name of person, group, or musician(s) commissioning the piece.
  • Contact info the person(s) that are involved in this commission (including people you would like to be CC or BCC in the email chain).
  • The instrumentation you would like the piece to be written for.
  • Approximate length and/or number of movements you would like.
  • Date of the premiere of the piece (if applicable).
  • Date you need/want the piece to be finished by (this may have to be negotiated for me to have enough time to work on the piece.
  • If I have your permission to publish the recording or video of the performance on my YouTube Channel, a music streaming service, and/or this website. You do not have to say yes to this to commission a piece, it is just helpful for me to get more commissions. You may also say yes or no to any combination of the previously mentioned uses.
  • Any additional information you would like me to know.
  • Acknowlegement that you have read the Terms and Conditions of Commissions and Usage and Copyright of my Work sections on the About page on this website.

I suggest you check out some of my other work and learn more about me in the About page of this webstie before commissioning a piece, so that you know if I am a good fit for your project. Thank you for your buisness!
