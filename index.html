Welcome!

Hello! My name is Noah Thompson and I am a student musician and composer. I specialize in writing romantic-inspired classical music with a focus on maintaining traditional musical forms and tonality. You can learn more about me in the "About" page on this website.

I post score animations and occassionly live performances of my pieces, as well as additional music-related content on my YouTube channel, N. JK. Thompson Music. I also officially publish all of my works on the International Music Score Library Project for free, under a CC BY-SA 4.0 liscense (essentially you can use my music for all purposes as long as you credit me).
