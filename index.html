Welcome

I prefer the style of the Romantic Era and hence my music tends to fall under either French or German romanticism with a bit a Baroque mixed in as well. All the sheet music for my piece can be found at the International Music Score Library Project (IMSLP). Score-animations (along with live performances) for all of my pieces can be found on my YouTube channel.
