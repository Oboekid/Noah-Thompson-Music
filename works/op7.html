Notes

Slavic Rhapsody was written for the 2022 National Young Composers’ Challenge. The piece was inspired by Rimsky-Korsakov’s Scheherazade and the overarching works of Antonin Dvorak. The work as a whole stems from Thompson’s fascination with Eastern European classical music in the romantic and post-romantic eras.
