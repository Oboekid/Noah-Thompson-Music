L'Étude de Colibri

Op. 6 | 3' | solo flute and piano | 2022
A short etude focusing on quintuplets for solo flute and piano.

Notes

This work was commissioned by flutist, Kalista Kaiser. The title of this piece translates into "The Humming Bird Study." This study is intended to be an exersize in quintuplets and being able to fluidly switch between sixteenths, eighths, triplets and quintuplets.
