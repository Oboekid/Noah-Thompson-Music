Serenade in C Major

Op. 4, No. 2 | 2' | 2 violins | 2021
Short classical-esque serenade for violin duet.

Score

  • Full score

  • Also available on IMSLP

Notes

The classical music YouTube Channel TwoSet Violin had a fan composition contest. This was written serenade in dedication to them and to hopefully be featured in their video. The first part of this duet was originally going to be in the third movement of Thompson's first violin concerto, but was moved here.
