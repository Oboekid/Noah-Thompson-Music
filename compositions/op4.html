Extra Information

The YouTube channel, TwoSet Violin, recently said they were going to be sharing fan compositions. I wrote this serenade in dedication to them and to hopefully be featured in their video. The first part of this duet was originally going to be in the third movement of my violin concerto, but I thought it would fit better here.
